About Dr. Ananya Sharma

Dr. Ananya Sharma is a board-certified cardiologist with over 12 years of experience in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. She completed her MD in Cardiology from AIIMS Delhi and is a Fellow of the American College of Cardiology (FACC).
